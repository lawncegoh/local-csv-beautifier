How it works

  1. Upload a CSV file or paste text.
  2. Pick cleanup options.
  3. Preview source and cleaned rows.
  4. Download your cleaned CSV and change log.

FAQ

Do my files upload to a server?

No. All transformations happen locally in your browser and the cleaned file is generated on-device.

Can I use it for large files?

Yes, within browser limits. For very large CSV files, try removing unnecessary columns first for best performance.

Can I reuse settings?

Yes, save and reload cleaning profiles in your browser for repeatable results.
